Huel

Description

Huel is a nutritionally complete food that is often used as a meal replacement.

Ingredients

  • Huel shaker bottle
  • 100g Huel powder
  • 610ml water

Steps

  1. Pour 610ml water into the shaker.

  2. Place 100 Huel powder into the shaker.

  3. Assemble and tighten the shaker very securely.

  4. Shake vigorously for 30-40 seconds.

  5. Optional: for best results, place in fridge for a few hours. Serve chilled.
