Tuna Pasta Bake

Description

Tuna pasta bake is a dish of pasta baked with sauce and tuna and topped with melted cheese.

Ingredients

  • 600g rigatoni
  • 50g butter
  • 50g plain flour
  • 600ml milk
  • 250 strong cheddar, grated
  • 2x160g cans tuna in spring water, drained
  • 330g can sweetcorn, drained
  • large handful chopped parsley
